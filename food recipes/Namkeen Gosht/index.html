Nameek Gosht Recipe

Mutton Namkeen Gosht karahi and must try this this bakra Eid.



Ingredients

  • Chicken mix boti 750g
  • -Adrak lehsan (Ginger garlic) crushed 2 tbs
  • -Himalayan pink salt 1 tsp or to taste
  • -Cooking oil 1/3 Cup
  • -Water ½ Cup or as required
  • -Dahi (Yogurt) whisked 1 Cup (room temperature)
  • -Hari mirch (Green chillies) 2-3++
  • -Kali mirch (Black pepper) crushed 1 tsp
  • Sabut dhania (Coriander seeds) crushed 1 tsp
  • -Safed mirch powder (White pepper powder) ½ tsp
  • -Zeera (Cumin seeds) roasted & crushed ½ tsp
  • -Chicken powder 1 tsp
  • -Coconut milk powder 1 tbs (optional)
  • -Lemon juice 2 tsp
  • -Adrak (Ginger) julienne 1 inch piece
  • -Olper’s Cream ¾ Cup (room temperature)
  • -Olper’s Cheddar cheese slices 3
  • -Garam masala powder ½ tsp
  • -Hara dhania (Fresh coriander) chopped
  • -Hari mirch (Green chilli) sliced
  • -Adrak (Ginger) julienne
